BAD ENDING (Lost someone really important)



I finished my time in that infirmary.
I already graduated from college and I end up NOT being a school doctor...
Instead、I work for a third-rate company.
I got bored at work one day (Most of the days、actually)
So I surfed the internet.

I suddenly remembered about that day...

The day when Riku ran outside the infirmary、naked.
I remember seeing his face... He was crying...

I was scared...

I was scared that I might use the word "love" wrongly
Because I wasn't sure if he knew that word himself...
I was scared that it would change about what he thinks about everything...

In the end I screwed everything up.

If only I was a bit more patient...
I wouldn't have to spend everyday of my life like this...

While they say that you shouldn't regret anything in life...
I'm sure I regret this one...

My life will continue to be gray

If it does anything though...
I would like to announce something.


I like you、Riku. No. I love you.

Even after a year、I love you.
And it only grows stronger everyday.

If only we can meet each other again...
So that I can properly say it to you、Riku...

Try again
