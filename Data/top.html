This is a visual novel game!

How to play:

Click on the picture above to start.

Click anywhere in the box to continue reading.

Actions will be flashed within a period of time.

Choose an action before the time runs out!

Not clicking on any of the choices can also be an action.

You can also click the "Skip to Action" button to go directly to the choices.

There are different kinds of endings that you can get depending on the actions you choose.



Enjoy!
